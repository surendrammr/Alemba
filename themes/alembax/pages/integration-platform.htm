Alemba Service Manager offers an advanced integration platform, which allows you to link together your IT management tools and systems.

There is a range of connectors that act as an interface between the Alemba Service Manager integration platform capabilities and third-party software applications, including SolarWinds, SNOW, Microsoft SCCM, Atlassian Jira and many more.

With the Alemba Service Manager integration platform, you can:

Automate the transfer of information

(for the resolution of calls or the completion of tasks, for example)

Manage alerts across different systems

Populate the Alemba Service Manager Core CMDB with externally discovered resources
