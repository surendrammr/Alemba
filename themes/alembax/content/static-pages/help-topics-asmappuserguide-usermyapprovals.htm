Viewing your Approvals in the ASM app (User login)

You can access your approvals by tapping the tile on the Workload (home) screen. You will initially see a list of all of your outstanding approvals.

Tap on an approval to see the details.

In this version of the app, the fields are static and custom screen designs are not rendered. Only plain text is supported; images and HTML formatting will not be displayed.

From here, you can view the history, add a note, approve or reject the approval.
