The Information Panel

All details windows have an information panel to the right of the main window, giving further information about the details on screen.

You can display it by selecting the button at the top right of the details window.

Typically, the Info panel displays the main details of the item in summary form, as well as the Analyst who created it, the date on which it was created, and any update details .
