Updating Your Contact Details

You can update your contact details within Self Service Portal.

  1. From the My Options menu, select Self Administration, and then Update Contact Details.
  2. In the Update your Contact Details page, check and update the stored details, as necessary.
  3. Click OK. A confirmation appears to acknowledge you have successfully updated your details.
