ASM app Home Screen (Analyst login)

The home screen appears once you have logged in and provides a quick overview of items that require your attention.

The tiles display the following information.

My Calls

The number of calls that are currently assigned to you, excluding calls that have been forwarded to another analyst or group

Forwarded to Me

The number of calls that have been forwarded to you

Forwarded to My Groups

The total number of calls that have been forwarded to your groups

Forwarded Internally

The number of calls that you have forwarded internally

Tap the required tile to display your Call List for that category.

Refreshing your Screen

To refresh the current call list, select the button . This button appears on the home and call lists screens.

Logging out

To log out of the app, select the menu and then the Logout button .
