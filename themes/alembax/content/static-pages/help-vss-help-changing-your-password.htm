Changing Your Password

Self Service Portal enables you to change your password within the self administration section.

  1. From the My Options menu, select Self Administration, and then Change Password.
  2. In the Change Your Password page, type your old password, and then type and confirm your new password, remembering that passwords are case sensitive.
  3. Click OK. A confirmation appears to acknowledge you have successfully updated your password.
