Reviewing Approvals

You can search for and view past and current approvals in Self Service Portal. Past approvals are approvals that were raised some time in the past and have been closed (approvals that have been rejected or approved). Current approvals are approvals that are pending a decision.

  1. From the My Options menu, select Approvals, and then select the Review Past/Current Approvals option.
  2. The Review Past/ Current Approvals page appears. Complete as many of the details as necessary.
  3. If you are looking for a specific approval, type its number in the Approval No field. Otherwise leave this field blank.
  4. Select the Approval State.

    5. Active Approvals to search for and return results on approvals that are currently open
    Completed < 1 Week to search for and return results on approvals that were implemented and completed in the last week
    Completed < 4 Weeks to search for and return results on all approvals that were implemented and completed in the last 4 weeks
  5. Click to display the Approval Summary.
