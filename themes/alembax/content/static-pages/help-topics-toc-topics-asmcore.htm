ASM Core

ASM Core offers a wide range of functionality which makes managing the Service Desk easier and more effective.

This section of the online help will document management of services and items, people, knowledge and bulletins.

It will also tell you how to use the audit trail feature to track data changes - what the change was, who made it and why.
