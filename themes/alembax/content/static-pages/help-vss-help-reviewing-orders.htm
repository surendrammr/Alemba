Reviewing Orders

You can review any orders (submitted or not submitted) in Self Service Portal.

Locate the My Orders widget on the home page. Then select one to view the detail.

Alternatively, review your past/current requests. Again, you can select one from the browse table to view the detail.

When you review the details, you can add a note or attach an object.
