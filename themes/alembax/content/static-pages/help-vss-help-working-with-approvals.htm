Working with Approvals

Within the Review & Action Approvals section, there are menu options that enable you, depending on your user permissions, to:

  • Action Outstanding Approvals. You can action and monitor approval tasks within Self Service Portal.
  • Review Past/ Current Approvals. The search facility enables you to review past and current Approvals. You are able to monitor the progress of approval tasks that have been assigned to you, all approval tasks that have been assigned to your organization and all approval tasks that belong to other organizations that you have permission to view.
  • Add Note to Your Approval. Additional information such as a note can be added to approval tasks.
  • Attach Object to Your Approval. You can also attach an object (also known as a file) to approval tasks.

Users and analysts linked to users can view, search for, or action approvals via the Portal.
