Escalation States

Escalation states for SLM events are displayed on the Service window. They alert you about impending escalations and breaches on events configured for the agreement applied to a call.

Escalation states are also displayed on the Calls Outstanding and Requests Outstanding windows.

According to an SLA applicable on a call, the user must receive the first call back within 3 hours of the call being logged. We can set an escalation event for every hour if the call back has not been made. Therefore, the SLM section will display three escalations and one breach if the first call back does not occur within 3 hours.

The following escalation states are associated with SLAs, OLAs, and UCs, and three with First Call Back (FCB), Response (Resp.), and Resolve events.

What it means
