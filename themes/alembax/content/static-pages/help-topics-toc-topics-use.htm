Use

We offer a range of products to meet the needs of our customers. Select the relevant link to access the section of the documentation that interests you.

ASM Core

This is used largely by system administrators, and people in the organization who are responsible for configuring the system, creating users and CMDB items, developing workflows and establishing security and self service portal settings.

It is also used by service desk analysts processing calls and requests on behalf of users.
Self Service Portal

Also known as the Self Service Portal, Self Service Portal allows end users to log calls and requests, and place orders.

They can also monitor existing calls, requests and orders, and, if they have the correct permissions, approve requests.
Nano Nano is a browser-agnostic interface for occasional and inexperienced users to manage calls, requests, tasks and approvals. It is a relatively new addition to the Alemba® range, and is growing in functionality with each release.
ASM Rapid Start Rapid Start offers a quick and easy installation solution. It delivers a set of pre-configured data and configurations that are designed to get you started with the key processes that you need to move from Service Transition to Service Operation.
ASMApps

With Alemba®’s ASM app, users can access their calls, approvals, orders and asset lists from a mobile device. They can add notes and view the history of the call, action approvals, and view orders and assets.
