Logging Out of ASM Core

It’s important to log out of the system correctly to ensure any column or color settings (on your Calls Outstanding, Requests Outstanding or Tasks Outstanding windows) are retained the next time you log in.

To log out of ASM Core, select ≡ and then Exit.

This ensures that any open windows (except any Call Details, Request Details or Task Details windows) will automatically re-open next time you log in to ASM Core. If you are on a Details window, ASM Core will prompt you to save your changes before you exit.
