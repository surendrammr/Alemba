Using the Online Help

The online help is designed to answer any questions you have on how to use ASM Core. You can use it in several ways:

Search boxes in the Home and topic help windows allow you to search on specific keywords.

The drop-down menu at the top of every help window contains options reflecting different aspects of the system. Selecting one of these options will lead you to further windows from which you can select more specific help.

Side menus to the right of the text within topics list related topics.

Links, which are highlighted in pink, appear throughout the Online Help.

Expanding text offers additional information that may not be relevant to every reader. Simply click on the arrow, or the text itself, to expand it. And then click on it again to collapse it once more.

Screenshots are depicted throughout the online help, to illustrate the screen you should be using, and selections and actions needed.

Videos, giving you an overview of new features, and brief tutorials on how to use different features in the system.

The online help appears in its own window. You can minimize this, or keep it open while you carry out the instructions.
