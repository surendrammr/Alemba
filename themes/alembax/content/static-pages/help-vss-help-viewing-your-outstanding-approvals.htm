Viewing Your Outstanding Approvals

You can view and action User Approval Task(s) that have been forwarded to you in Self Service Portal.

Analysts to whom approval tasks have been forwarded cannot view or action these tasks via Self Service Portal.

  1. From the My Options menu, select Approvals, and then select the Outstanding Approvals option.
  2. The Approvals Awaiting Your Action page appears, showing a summary of all of your outstanding approvals in the Approval Summary browse table.
  3. Click on an approval to see the Approval Details page.
  4. From here, you can use the buttons at the top of the page to Approve, Reject, Add a Note or Add an Attachment.
  5. If you choose to approve or reject the approval, you may be prompted for your password, and given the opportunity to add comments to the approval/rejection.
  6. When you have finished, click on Complete to complete the task, or Cancel to return to the Details page.
  7. If you complete the approval/rejection, your task is updated with the approval decision. If you approve the task, this will close the task in ASM Core. Approval tasks are generally part of a workflow plan for change. Your approval decision (acceptance or rejection) will determine the next step of the workflow.
