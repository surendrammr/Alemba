Logging and Reviewing Requests

You can submit requests via Self Service Portal and, depending on your system setup, assign them to support analysts or groups. You can also search for past requests (requests that have been resolved), current requests (requests that are open) and monitor the progress of submitted requests. 

Additional information such as a note or a file (object) can also be added to your request.

You can also view requests that have been scheduled for implementation within a specified month or view reports that have been generated.

You can:

  • Log a Request
  • Review Past/ Current Requests
  • Add a Note to Your Request
  • Attach Object to Your Request
  • View Forward Schedule of Change
  • View Change Windows

Your user role will determine your permissions and access to the above functions.
