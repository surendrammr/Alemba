What is Service Automation?

Service management automation is the strategic use of technology, such as ESM and ITSM software, to streamline and automate service delivery and maintenance processes. By eliminating manual effort in routine tasks, organizations achieve faster response times, fewer errors, and greater efficiency - driving operational agility and innovation.
