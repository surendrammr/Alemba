Stay Connected Anytime, Anywhere with Alemba Service Manager's Responsive Design

Alemba Service Manager offers a fully responsive design, empowering end users and support agents to manage their workload seamlessly from mobile and tablet devices. Whether you're tracking tickets, handling requests, viewing metrics, or generating reports, the mobile and tablet versions of ASM provide full functionality just like on a desktop or laptop. The mobile self-service catalog and ASM Nano interface allow agents and users to stay productive 24/7, even while on the go.
