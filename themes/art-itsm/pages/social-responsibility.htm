Alemba’s Commitment to Social Responsibility

At Alemba, we believe businesses should positively impact society and the environment. As a global ITSM software company, we embed social responsibility into everything we do.

Supporting Communities

We collaborate with customers in the charity sector and local government to:

  • Provide Cash Donations: Supporting impactful charitable causes.
  • Share Expertise: Hosting IT workshops for non-profits and local governments.
  • Support Local Causes: Contributing to local initiatives that strengthen the IT and technology sectors.

Sustainable Practices

Sustainability is at our core. We’ve consciously reduced waste by:

  • Rethinking Trade Show Giveaways: Donating to charity for every trade show attendee instead of giving away swag.
  • Donating Equipment: Refurbishing and donating old IT equipment to those in need.

Our Commitment in Action:

Plan International

At the Pink25 Conference in Las Vegas and the DigiGov24 Expo in London, we donated to Plan International, a globally recognized organization advancing children’s rights and equality for girls in over 80 countries, reducing plastic merchandise.

Find out more about Plan International: www.plan-international.org

Supporting Staff-Led Fundraising

We proudly support our team members in their personal charitable efforts – from sponsorship to donations. Most recently, we were thrilled to back our Head of Global Business Development, James Speight’s three half marathon challenge, making the final donation to help him cross the finish line for FORCE Cancer Charity.

Celebrating Local Heroes

Alemba was proud to sponsor two categories at the 2025 Luton Borough Council Excellence Awards: Rising Star of the Year and Dare to Try. These awards celebrate the innovation and ambition that drive public service forward – values we’re honoured to champion.
