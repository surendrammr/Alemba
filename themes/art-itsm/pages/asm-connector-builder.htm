What is the Connector Builder?

The GUI-driven CMDB Connector Builder in ASM enables the development of custom CMDB connectors, facilitating seamless integration with external auto-discovery applications and repository databases.

This makes building a federated CMDB a realistic prospect for ASM customers by supporting a cost-effective method to build a single pane of glass view of their infrastructure.

The CMDB Connector Builder can be used to create and manage integrations with your auto-discovery tools.

Benefits

  • Custom Configuration: Tailor your custom CMDB connector to your specific needs, mapping any or all information stored in your auto-discovery tools or repositories.
  • Seamless External Integration: Connect with external auto-discovery applications to build the ASM CMDB to model your infrastructure.
  • Dependency Mapping: Import the relationships between Configuration Items (CIs), services and systems, building a complete picture of your infrastructure.
  • Support Change Management: Create an invaluable model to support an understanding of the risks of each Change you make.
  • Problem Management Insights: Use the CMDB dependency map to provide insights for dependent infrastructure that may be causing related errors.
  • Incident Management Accuracy: Improve the accuracy of call logging with real CIs assigned to each Incident record, allowing CI-specific knowledge articles to support call resolution.
