100% License Free

Alemba Service Manager offers an attractive, user-friendly self-service interface that allows end users to log calls and requests, and place orders.

Alemba Service Manager Self Service is 100% license-free, which means you can make it available to as many users as you wish without incurring any additional expenses.

Custom branding

The self-service interface can be branded to your corporate logos and color themes.

Chat

Users can chat with analysts in real time to help solve their problems.

Shop

Customers can browse the service request catalogue and easily request services by adding them to their shopping cart.

Solve

Your customers can solve problems by searching the integrated knowledge base.

Widgets

Design custom ‘get in touch’ widgets for easy access to frequently requested services.

News

The news feed function allows you to share important information with your customers.

Mobile

A responsive UI allows users to log and track calls, approvals, orders and assets while on the go.

For all business units

You can adopt a unified self-service portal incorporating different departments, such as IT HR and Facilities, or choose to have segregated portals for each business unit.
