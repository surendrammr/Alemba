Manage Change Without Disruption

Managing IT change doesn’t have to mean risking disruption. Alemba’s Change, Release, and Deployment Management solution provides a structured, automated approach to delivering updates and improvements across your IT environment, without compromising service stability.

ITIL 4-Aligned Change, Release, and Deployment Practices

Alemba Service Manager supports best-practice processes defined in ITIL 4:

Change Enablement

ensures every change is assessed for risk, authorized appropriately, and implemented with minimal disruption.

Deployment Management

controls how service components are deployed across development, test, and production environments, guaranteeing consistency and reducing failure rates.

Release Management

coordinates the availability of new or updated services so they deliver value when needed.

Alemba is PinkVERIFY™ certified for all three practice areas.

Key Capabilities of Alemba Service Manager

Zero-Code Workflow Automation

Design and automate the entire change lifecycle, from request to post-implementation review, with a visual drag-and-drop workflow engine. Handle standard, normal, and emergency changes without writing code. Define risk-based approvals, task sequences, and audit gates in minutes.

Live Change Calendar and Forward Schedule

Plan changes with confidence using a colour-coded Release Calendar and live Forward Schedule of Change. Track dependencies, coordinate release windows, and avoid change collisions across your service ecosystem.

Risk-Aware Approvals and CMDB Integration

Changes are linked to configuration items (CIs) in the CMDB, enabling automated risk scoring and visual impact analysis. Approvers can assess the full impact of a change before authorizing its implementation.

Version-Controlled Release Management

Coordinate releases across environments with version-controlled packages. Trigger scripts, use Alemba Integration Hub playbooks, or launch Azure DevOps pipelines via REST APIs to ensure consistent deployments.

Self-Service Change Requests

Empower business stakeholders to submit and approve change requests through an intuitive portal. Submissions are intelligently routed based on risk level, service type, or CI linkage, improving transparency and reducing IT bottlenecks.

Built-in Reporting and KPIs

Track performance metrics in real time. Dashboards show change success rates, failure trends, and deployment frequency. Data exports integrate with Power BI for advanced reporting and insights.

Seamless DevOps and Toolchain Integration

Alemba integrates with Jira, Jenkins, SCCM (now known as Microsoft Endpoint Configuration Manager), and other monitoring platforms, as well as various tools, utilizing REST APIs. Automate handoffs across your CI/CD pipeline and introduce shift-left controls to detect risks earlier in the development lifecycle.
