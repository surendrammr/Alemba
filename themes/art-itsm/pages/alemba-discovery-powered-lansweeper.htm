Gain Complete Visibility. Improve IT Service Management.

Alemba Discovery powered by Lansweeper automates IT asset discovery, providing real-time insight into your IT landscape. Integrated with Alemba Service Manager (ASM), it ensures service requests, incidents, and changes are based on the most current infrastructure data. The result? A single source of truth that improves efficiency, reduces downtime, and enhances service quality.

Benefits

  • Smarter IT Asset Management (ITAM)
    • Automatically identify and track IT and non-IT networked assets.
    • Optimize software licensing and reduce unnecessary repurchases.
    • Control cloud environments to prevent costly server sprawl.
  • Faster Incident Resolution
    • Access real-time configuration item (CI) data for accurate incident logging.
    • Reduce resolution times with immediate insight into dependencies and history.
  • Reliable Change & Release Management
    • Reduce service disruptions with clear visibility into CI relationships.
    • Automate risk assessment and improve change approvals.
    • Ensure compliance with complete traceability and audit trails.

How it Works

Alemba Discovery leverages Lansweeper's advanced discovery engine to automate CMDB population.

  • Agent & Agentless Discovery: Maximum flexibility for comprehensive asset tracking.
  • Discrepancy Engine: Detect unauthorized infrastructure changes.
  • Cloud & Non-IT Asset Discovery: Identify all IP-connected devices.
  • Automated Software License Management: Compare purchased vs. deployed assets to optimize spending.

Real-time visualisation of CI relationships and their relation to services, supporting the Incident Management and Change Management processes in ASM.

Populate your CMDB with the important information that supports your Service Management processes, reducing time to resolve issues and minimizing failed changes.
